What is InPartnership

InPartnership Points is the way in which prizes will be allocated in our Sales Heroes Academy campaign.

InPartnership is a points based scheme, allowing winners to ‘spend’ their allocated prize points on items in the online InPartnership store.

  • How can I win?

How can I win?

From 1st August – 31st October 2016, your NetApp Academy activity will win you Power Points. Use your Power Points to graduate each level of the NetApp Academy and win some big prizes!. The more power points you achieve, the more chances there are of winning big!

You will receive points for successfully completing any of the following missions:

  • 1 Power Point
    per £10k closed revenue

  • 2 Power Points
    per accepted deal registration

  • 5 Power Points
    per £10k NetApp AFF closed revenue

  • 5 Power Points
    for running a marketing campaign from our VMM site or NetApp’s GRID

  • 10 Power Points
    for attending a NetApp event

You can make your claims directly through this app, in the ‘Make My Claim’ section, where you can also track your Superhero level and your total Power Points.

Prizes

Every time you successfully complete a NetApp Academy sales mission, make sure you claim your Power Points and you could win big!

1st – 1000 InPartnership Points (worth £500)

2nd – 700 InPartnership Points (worth £350)

3rd – 400 InPartnership Points (worth £200)

There will also be some spot prizes for graduating through the different levels of Hero Academy, so keep going!!
