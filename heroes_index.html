Welcome

Welcome Initiate to the NetApp Sales Heroes Academy. Have you got what it takes to graduate as a fully-fledged NetApp Sales Superhero?

It is your job to successfully complete a series of super hero challenges in order to accrue points. The more points you have the quicker you will advance through our 5 levels of Hero Academy:

  • Initiate

  • Apprentice

  • Side Kick

  • Hero

  • NetApp Sales Superhero

Your points will also earn you Superhero rewards! With instant prizes for graduating with a Superhero status and 3 major prizes quarterly to be won by the student who achieves the most points.

So do you think you have got what it takes to make it to graduation? Then we want you!

Complete the challenges. Gain your points. Become a hero!

What is InPartnership

InPartnership Points is the way in which prizes will be allocated in our Sales Heroes Academy campaign.

InPartnership is a points based scheme, allowing winners to ‘spend’ their allocated prize points on items in the online InPartnership store.

  • How can I win?

How can I win?

From 1st September – 31st December 2016, your NetApp Academy activity will win you Power Points. Use your Power Points to graduate each level of the NetApp Academy and win some big prizes! The more power points you achieve, the more chances there are of winning big!

You will receive points for successfully completing any of the following missions:

  • 1 Power Point
    per £10k closed revenue

  • 2 Power Points
    per accepted deal registration

  • 5 Power Points
    per £10k NetApp AFF closed revenue

  • 5 Power Points
    for running a marketing campaign from our VMM site or NetApp’s GRID

  • 10 Power Points
    for attending a NetApp event

You can make your claims directly through this app, in the ‘Make My Claim’ section, where you can also track your Superhero level and your total Power Points.
